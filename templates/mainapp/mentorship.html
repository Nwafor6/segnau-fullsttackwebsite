UNIVERSITIES/CHAPTERS MENTORSHIP CIRCLE

Chapter and Universities Mentorship Circle is a program designed to mentor chapters to harness the benefits of SEG in their institutions. This program began on the 22nd of August 2021 with 5 universities in the South-east Nigeria but has increased to 13 universities from Nigeria, Cameroun and Azerbaijan. This program began after the Local Challenge Bowl and has been active till date. The aim of the mentorship program is to propagate SEG among university in order create new chapters and to help re-activate dormant chapters in Nigeria. SEG SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION REVIEW PROGRAM

This is a series of postworkshop virtual and physical mentorship to guide interested applicants from both the chapter and other chapters through the application process of the SEG scholarship application. This led to increase in SEG Scholarship awareness in Nigerian public universities.
